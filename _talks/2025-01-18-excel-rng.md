This lecture covers the fundamentals and applications of quantum random number generation, explaining how quantum mechanical principles enable the generation of certified random numbers superior to classical methods.

**Key Topics**
- Classical vs. quantum random number generation
- Quantum sources of randomness
- Device-independent randomness certification
- Applications in cryptography and simulation
- Entropy expansion techniques

**Context:** Part of an ATAL sponsored program on quantum computing and AI applications.
