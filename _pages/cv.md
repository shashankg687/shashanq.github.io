### Recent Publications (2024-2025)

#### 1. **ChaQra: A Cellular Unit of the Indian Quantum Network** (2024)

**Published:** Scientific Reports, Volume 14, Article 16752 (2024)

**Authors:** S. Gupta, I. Agarwal, V. Mogiligidda, R. Kumar Krishnan, S. Chennuri, and team

**Key Findings:**
- Novel cellular quantum network architecture for India
- Derived from Sanskrit "Chakra" (circular topology)
- Scalable to cover entire Indian subcontinent

**Core Features:**
1. **Crypto Agility:** Integration with existing telecom fibers
2. **Software-Defined Networking (SDN):** Dynamic routing between nodes
3. **Reliability:** Hybrid quantum-safe cryptography against DoS attacks
4. **Upgradability:** Modular design for technological advancement
5. **Beyond QKD:** Distributed computing and multi-party computation

**Technical Specifications:**
- Point-to-Multipoint architecture
- Hub-and-spoke topology with trusted relay nodes
- Wavelength division multiplexing (WDM) coexistence
- AI-enabled network control
- Scalable to multiple hubs and spokes

**Significance:**
- First comprehensive plan for India's quantum-secure network
- Addresses national quantum mission objectives
- Practical deployment-ready architecture
- Foundation for quantum-secure Indian subcontinent
